About Pricing ?

The slider above doubles as a display for the current amount of RAM and threads that the server has along with a price calculator. Additional threads can be purchased for about $5 but the price is dependant on the amount of RAM. Also for every 2 GB of RAM an extra thread is added for free which is why there isn't an option to calculate the extra thread cost.
